## Naming Criteria Used

1. **Memorable** — Can you say it once and remember it?
2. **Meaningful** — Does it hint at social proof / testimonials / transformation?
3. **Domain available** — .com strongly preferred (.io acceptable)
4. **No existing brands** — Verified no company/product name conflicts
5. **SaaS-appropriate** — Modern, clean, works as a brand
6. **Short** — Under 12 characters ideal

## My Recommendation

**ProofBloom** is the strongest option. Here's why:
- ✅ .com available
- ✅ "Proof" establishes the category instantly
- ✅ "Bloom" perfectly captures the core value prop (one testimonial blooming into 5+ assets)
- ✅ Unique — no existing products
- ✅ Visual branding potential (flower/bloom motif, growth imagery)
- ✅ 10 characters, easy to say and type
- ✅ Works across contexts: "I used ProofBloom to turn my testimonials into case studies"

**Runner-up: ProofTurn** — more action-oriented, directly describes the transformation pipeline.
