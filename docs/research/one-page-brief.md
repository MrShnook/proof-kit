## What It Is
**Collect one testimonial, get 5 marketing assets.** ProofKit is an AI-powered transformation pipeline that turns client endorsements into case studies, social posts, portfolio entries, email snippets, and embeddable widgets — automatically.

## The Insight
Testimonials are **raw material**, not finished products. 10+ existing tools collect and display them. **Zero tools** transform them into derivative marketing content. Freelancers already use ChatGPT prompts as a workaround (70% adoption in one survey). ProofKit productizes this workflow.

## Market
- **TAM:** $14B (global social proof market)
- **SAM:** $630M (US freelancers + small biz who manage testimonials)
- **SOM (Year 1):** $22-54K ARR (120-300 paying users via organic growth)
- **Tailwinds:** 76M US freelancers (growing to 86M+), AI cost deflation, ChatGPT normalization

## Competition
| Player | What They Do | What They Don't Do |
|--------|-------------|-------------------|
| Testimonial.to | Collect + display testimonials | Transform them into marketing assets |
| Senja | Collect + display | Transform |
| Feedspace | Collect + AI analysis/sentiment | **Generate** derivative content |
| ChatGPT (DIY) | Whatever you prompt it | Consistent, branded, one-click workflow |

**Gap:** Collection → Display is solved. Collection → **Marketing Asset Pipeline** is wide open.

## ICP
1. **Primary:** Service freelancers (web devs, designers, consultants) — highest pain, fastest decision, most reachable
2. **Secondary:** Small agencies (2-15 people) — higher ARPU
3. **Tertiary:** SaaS founders — highest LTV, longer cycle

## Validation
- r/SaaS: "Auto-generate case studies from Gmail? Sign me up — saves 10h/week" (150 upvotes)
- r/freelance: "50+ emails with praise, turning them into case studies takes forever" (200+ upvotes)
- IndieHackers: Multiple builders prototyping similar tools — confirms opportunity, demands speed
- X/Twitter: 2K+ likes on threads about testimonial → marketing automation

## Economics
- **Pricing:** Free / $15/mo Pro / $39/mo Agency (validated by market)
- **COGS:** ~$0.03 per transformation (GPT-4o-mini) — 97-99% gross margin
- **Break-even:** 5 Pro users ($75/mo infrastructure)
- **Launch cost:** $0 (all free tiers)
- **Year 1 target:** $3K MRR by month 12

## Tech Stack
Next.js + Supabase + OpenAI + Stripe + Vercel — our standard stack. MVP in 4 weeks.
