# ProofKit — Venture Context

## What It Is
Collect one testimonial, get 5 marketing assets — case studies, social posts, portfolio entries, and embeddable widgets auto-generated by AI.

## Problem
Freelancers and small businesses struggle to leverage testimonials effectively. They collect a quote, maybe display it on a page, and that's it. The raw material — a genuine customer endorsement — could be transformed into dozens of marketing assets, but nobody has the time or skill to do it manually.

## Core Insight
Testimonials are **raw material**, not finished products. The transformation pipeline (testimonial → case study, social post, portfolio card, email snippet, landing page widget) is where the real value lies. Nobody automates this.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Freelancers and solopreneurs (73M+ in the US alone)
- **Secondary:** Small agencies (5-20 employees)
- **Tertiary:** SaaS companies doing customer marketing

## Competitive Landscape (Initial)
Testimonial.to, Senja, Shoutout, Boast all focus on **collection + display**. None auto-generate derivative marketing assets. This is the gap.

## Pricing (Hypothesized)
- Free: 3 testimonials, basic text assets
- Pro ($15/mo): Unlimited testimonials, all asset types, custom branding
- Agency ($39/mo): Multi-client, white-label, team access
